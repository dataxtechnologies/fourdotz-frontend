Payout timeline
How your funds reach you
Payment received
Your owners or tenants pay maintenance fees and dues. We collects and confirms the payment.
Some days
Amount reflects in your available balance
Verification & holding
Fourdotz verifies the received payment. The amount is held for 3 days before it becomes eligible for payout.
3-day hold
Payout request unlocks after day 3
Payout request
Once the holding period is over, you can raise a payout request for the full or partial available balance.
After day 3
Full or partial payout supported
Approval & bank credit
Fourdotz admin reviews your request. Once approved, the amount is credited directly to your registered bank account.
3–5 business days
Status updates: Approved → Completed

Total timeline: From payment confirmation to bank credit — allow up to 8–9 business days (3-day verification hold + 3–5 days post-approval processing). You will be notified at each stage. Super admin uploads UTR / screenshot / reference ID on completion.
